Projects


Fake Smile

Fake Smile is our first video which was done in collaboration with Headspace254, an organization which aims to raise mental health awareness within the creative community. The video aims to bring out the prception of mental disorders by African communites and most importantly crash the stigma around the topic in a form most people find easy to relate with, art.




Rooted

Rooted is an annual anniversary celebration of Mizizi Afriqa's inception.



Brikicho-Seek the hidden

Brikicho is the swahili name for the game "Hide and Seek" and the events basically incoparates Poetry , Music and Theatre to form a social commentary on issues that are mostly hidden from the odinary citizens eyes but affectects them in their day to day lives.

These Issues are selected and curated from various forms of "normalised" Human Rights Abuses that occurs round The Country.



Art Mtaani

Art Mtaani mtaani is a community based event whose main target audience is the Youth. The event aims to discover new talents from the grassroots and nature them into artists that have a voice in the society. It also includes Themed Editions that a geared towards invoking Social Dilouge on Societal Matters.

The long term goal is to see Art Mtaani evolve into a fully Fledged Community Festival.



Jiachilie

"Jiachilie" loosely translates to "let it out", and as the name suggests, the event's main purpose is to provide a safe space for people to talk about and exchange their sentiments on Mental Health Matters and also connect the audience to proffesional services if need be.

The Event is in patnership With Mental 360.
