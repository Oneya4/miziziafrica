Bio

Mizizi Afriqa is a collection of individual artists who overtime have grown to share a bond stronger than friendship. Here's what makes them well, them. From top...


Robert is a visionary creative, graphics designer, a thinker and most importantly a human. He is among the founders of Mizizi Afriqa. He works as a poet and writer in the group and has been a grandslam finalist. He is generally an advocate of humanity.


Elizabeth is a singer, poet and a writer at Mizizi Afriqa. She likes singing, listening to music, reading novels, cooking and making new friends. She studies a bachelor degree in commerce specializing in procurement.Her goal is to exploit her potential in everything she does hence the mantra "Keep on keeping on"


Kinaya, the Phenom Will RISE!. That is embeded in his name and his work. He works as a poet and writer at Mizizi Afriqa. This mantra guides him and gives him reason to continue provoking a positive thought process to those of free spirit. Home is where the art lives, may its kingdom come.


Faustine is a creative at Mizizi Afriqa. He is a writer, poet and graphics designer. He likes listening to music and exploring different forms of self expression. His goal is to add layers of mortar where others lay the brick


O'neya is a creative working at Mizizi Afriqa. He is a programmer and a guitarist. He finds his escape in performing arts when everything is seemingly going downhill. He envisions a world where people are mindful of the impact their actions have on others. "Don't be yourself, be the kind and best version of yourself."
